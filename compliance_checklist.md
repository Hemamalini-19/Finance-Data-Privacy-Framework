Privacy by Design: Is the data processing limited to the specific purpose of the finance application?

Security of Processing: Are cloud-native encryption standards (AES-256) applied to financial data at rest?

Data Subject Rights: Does the system allow for the automated export or deletion of client data upon request?

Access Control: Is there a documented matrix showing which business units have access to sensitive financial PII?

Transparency: Is there a clear privacy notice available for stakeholders and customers?
